Welcome to the Colenso Project

Welcome to the home of the Colenso project. This website is a user friendly repository of letters and other documents relating to William Colenso. Each document is stored in the TEI format and is available for download.

To get started you can browse all of the documents or begin a search


William Colenso

Botanist

William Colenso was a Cornish Christian missionary to New Zealand, and also a printer, botanist, explorer and politician. He attended the signing of the Treaty of Waitangi and later wrote an account of the events at Waitangi.
—Wikipedia

Born: November 7, 1811, Penzance, United Kingdom

Died: February 10, 1899, Napier
